Why Choose Us

Your Success is Our Success

e-Sago Capital's can only be successful if its clients are successful. The business model was built delibaretly to ensure that the company focuses on thier clients success as it translates to our success.

  • Our crowdfunding platfrom aims to provide access to capital for over 5000 start-up companies raising more than P2 Billion by 2030

What We Do

Our Services

Crowd
Funding

Crowdfunding will facilitate the matching of entrepreneuer with investors. Our platform will connect individuals and institutions alike, providing investors the opportunity to support the businesses they like, and growing together with them

Corporate
Advisory

Our focus area is on social enterprises. Our team will play a fund-raising advisory role to enterprises that are predominantly involved in acheiving sustainable development goals. We aim to become a thought leader and practitioner in mobilizing capital targetted towards the SDG's

Mortgage
Administration

Special Purpose Alliances will be created to bring technical expertise for administering social housing mortgages for the underhoused.In partnership with entities that are looking to provide financing in a sustainable manner for their member base, we will work with Pension Funds in structuring an effective mechanism of providing home financing.
